[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I watch the candlelight upon your skin
Open my arms and let the night begin
A heavy glance, a soft and pulling tide
I breathe you all in, with nowhere left to hide

[Verse 1]
The wooden floor is warm beneath our feet
I hear your pulse, a slow and steady beat
You lean your head to rest upon my chest
I know this hour is what I love the best

[Pre-Chorus]
Every sigh you give is pulling me
Into the dark, where I am meant to be
I feel the heat between us rising higher
We are a spark that turns into a fire

[Chorus]
So let me breathe you all in, taking my time
Tasting the sweet on your lips, crossing the line
Holding you close as the shadows begin
Closing my eyes, gonna breathe you all in

[Post-Chorus]
Breathe you all in, making you mine
Breathe you all in, crossing the line

[Verse 2]
A gentle wind is blowing through the door
But I just want to love you more and more
The amber glow is dancing in your eyes
I found my heaven underneath these skies

[Pre-Chorus 2]

Into the deep, where I am meant to be
I feel the heat between us rising higher
We are a spark that turns into a fire

[Chorus]
So let me breathe you all in, taking my time
Tasting the sweet on your lips, crossing the line
Holding you close as the shadows begin
Closing my eyes, gonna breathe you all in

[Post-Chorus 2]
Breathe you all in, making you mine
Breathe you all in, crossing the line

[Bridge]
The world outside is fading fast
But what we have is built to last
I pull you in, we lose control
This love from Saeed will fill your soul
We lock the doors and kill the light
We own the magic of the night

[Spoken]
I just want to stay right here, tangled up with you
No rushing, no running, just us

[Final Chorus]
So let me breathe you all in, taking my time
